Research-focused system to stitch thousands of underwater frames into accurate 2D mosaics and support 3D reconstruction. Designed to handle noisy, low-contrast underwater images and produce high-quality panoramas for archaeological documentation.

Tools: Python, OpenCV, SIFT / ORB, FLANN, RANSAC, CLAHE, MATLAB
Roles & Responsibilities:
  • Collected and preprocessed 3,690+ underwater frames; applied denoising, color correction and CLAHE for contrast improvement.
  • Implemented feature detection & matching pipeline (SIFT/ORB + FLANN) with RANSAC homography estimation.
  • Designed sliding-window stitching and multi-band blending for seam reduction and realistic panoramas.
  • Validated outputs using SSIM and iterative parameter tuning for underwater conditions.
